Sahayaq is an AI-powered platform that transforms scattered community data into real-time action by connecting NGOs and volunteers efficiently.

NGOs can upload reports or requests about local needs, which are then analyzed using AI to identify the type, location, and urgency of the issue. These needs are organized and visualized clearly, enabling quick decision-making and prioritization.

On the other side, volunteers receive relevant tasks based on their location and availability. They can easily accept tasks, view details, and contribute, making the response process faster and more effective.

By automating data processing and intelligently matching resources, Sahayaq reduces delays, improves coordination, and ensures that help reaches the right people at the right time.
